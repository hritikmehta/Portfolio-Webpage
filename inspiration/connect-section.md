# Connect Section Inspiration

## Goal
Design a clean final contact block aligned with the hand-drawn concept.

## Structure
- Primary email callout
- Message textarea/form
- Platform links row (GitHub, LinkedIn, X, Medium)

## Suggested Content
- Headline: "Mail me at <email> or drop a message"
- Short response-time expectation line
- CTA button: Send Message

## Visual Notes
- Keep generous whitespace.
- Use bordered inputs and platform pills for consistency.
- This section should feel quieter than previous sections.

## Responsive Notes
- Stack platform links in a 2x2 or 1-column grid on small screens.
- Keep form controls full-width.
