# Career Section Inspiration

## Goal
Build a timeline-first career section inspired by the hand-drawn sketches.

## Structure
- Sticky section-aware nav marker (Career active).
- Timeline cards with period ranges and role highlights.
- CTA for résumé download.
- Horizontal strip for tools/skills/social proof logos.

## Suggested Content Blocks
1. **Timeline Header**
   - Title: Career Timeline
   - Subtitle: "From fundamentals to product-focused engineering"
   - CTA: Download Resume

2. **Milestone Cards**
   - IIT Kharagpur (2018–2023)
   - Housing.com (2023–Present)
   - Next milestone (placeholder)

3. **Evidence Bar**
   - Skills: React, TypeScript, Performance, System Design, Product Thinking

## Visual Notes
- Keep strong section separation using a top border and soft contrasting background.
- Maintain card rhythm with equal spacing and consistent corner radius.
- Active/current role card should have accent border.

## Responsive Notes
- Collapse timeline to a single-column stack on small screens.
- Move CTA below title for narrow widths.
